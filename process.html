Freeform is a multi-disciplinary studio based out of San Francisco.

Our work is primarily focused on residential projects that require a high level of attention to detail and execution, but are also involved in compelling commercial commissions.

We care about space - primarily great space.

Great design is the culmination of conversation. Conversation with our clients, the contractor, and the artisans. Conversation between space and material. Between reality and potential.

Great space, not unlike great art, is defined by the ability to create an emotional response. We strive to create compelling works that represent our clients vision and take advantage of the site's opportunities.
